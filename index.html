Weeekly

Weeekly is a South Korean girl group known for their bright, cheerful image and energetic performances. Managed by IST Entertainment, they debuted in 30th June 2020 and gained attention with their youthful charm and catchy music.

discography

As of 2024, Weeekly has released a total of 6 extended plays (EPs) and multiple singles. Their most recent comeback was with the EP "Bliss" in July 2024, following their earlier digital single "Stranger" and a fansong "Good Day (Special Dailee)" in 2023.

filmography

Weeekly has appeared in a variety of shows, including reality shows, variety programs, movie, dramas and some surival shows. These shows allow fans to see the more relaxed, fun side of Weeekly, as well as their hard work behind the scenes.

Weeekly has held several concerts and fanmeetings since their debut, allowing fans to connect with them both online and in person. These fanmeetings and concerts have helped Weeekly grow closer to their fans, solidifying their place in the K-pop industry as a rising group.
